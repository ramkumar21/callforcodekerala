How To use qaul.net

Open your wifi and select the qaul.net network.

Download the program for your device.



To run qaul.net on your device, you'll need administrative rights.
Android devices need to be rooted, iPhones need to be jailbreaked.

At startup, qaul.net configures your device and the network automatically.
